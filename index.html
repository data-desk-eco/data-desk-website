ESG is over — pick a side

Drawing on over 15 years’ experience in campaigning and journalism, the latest industry knowledge and a range of cutting-edge technical tools, we arm campaign groups with the stories they need to shift the dial on public opinion and the hard statistics required to convince policymakers of the case for change.

Our approach to finding and developing stories is unique. It involves a deep understanding of the key datasets — satellite imagery, commodity tracking, leaks and other alternative data sources — and the technical skills to tame and interrogate them.

We synthesise and simplify these data into dashboards and interactive documents that help us explore data with our clients and jointly develop the stories that matter.

What we do

  • Investigations — we find the stories that matter in difficult data. We have specialisms in the trade of climate-risk commodities: oil, coal, gas, beef and soy
  • Discovery — we gather hard-to-find data so that you can focus on analysis. We are experts in scouring satellite imagery, corporate records, trade data, leaks and lobbying filings
  • Exploration — we work in a unique way combining quick prototyping with analytical rigour letting you query and probe large and complex datasets to find your story
  • Strategy — we advise organisations on establishing a data investigations function and making better use of digital sources in their research
  • Training — we can train your teams in the skills they need to carry out in-depth investigative research with digital sources

Previous work

  • Spearheaded the first six months of Global Witness’s rapid response to Russia’s invasion of Ukraine, including a high-impact investigation into the supply chain for Russian military jet fuel, which triggered the sale of a significant joint venture stake and a pending war crimes investigation
  • Drove highly complex data investigations into deforestation, including a flagship report revealing thousands of hectares of illegal clearance in the supply chains of major Brazilian beef companies, resulting in the removal of suppliers from industry whitelists and an inquiry by the Norwegian Government Pension Fund
  • Achieved landmark reforms to the UK’s system of company registration through an influential campaign based on a unique, large scale analysis of the beneficial ownership register
  • Co-bylined on more than a dozen front-page stories in The Times of London over two years, including major investigations into tax havens, cyber-crime and government contracting
